Context

Why Meta built this new tool?

Finding - Advertiser Problem

Advertisers receiving high-volume messages can't keep up with manual replies, leading to lower ad conversion

10+ messages/day → response rate declines

Finding - Competitor Research

10% of advertisers pay for third-party tools to send bulk replies, re-engaging hundreds of customers at once

Proves the user need and revenue potential

Solution

Introduced a native bulk reply tool, making it accessible to all advertisers and monetizable for Meta

Tested MVP with 10% advertisers

Design Focus

Helped advertisers discover and adopt the tool through a simple, easy-to-learn experience

Scope: tool design 0→1, end-to-end flow

Learning

AI accelerates design but cannot replace human judgment

AI accelerates execution

Cut delivery from 3 months to 1 month by handling layout exploration, component generation, and prototyping.

AI cannot find where to focus

Scoping and prioritization required business context and user understanding that AI lacked.

AI cannot inform decisions

Trade-off reasoning, stakeholder alignment, and cross-team context needed human judgment.

AI lacks ecosystem understanding

Meta's inbox required knowledge of competing tools, design system constraints, and platform dynamics.
