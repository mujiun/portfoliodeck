Clarify Problems

My Approach

Led comprehensive research across three lenses to understand the ecosystem

Outcome

Incorporated insights to land a clear view on user journeys and pain points

Finding — Successful Journey

Users followed certain tasks, sequence, timing → fast value, trust to adopt/spend

They have more knowledge or support

Finding — Unsuccessful Journey

Users struggled to navigate and use tools → never found value to retain/spend

Various drop-off points — fixing a few wouldn't change the outcome

Solution — Redefine Onboarding

Meta to make this successful, value-discovery journey the standard path for everyone

Meta to own the end-to-end journey, 5 teams to collaborate

Solution — Artifact

Meta to build new linear guidance, helping users find value and build the trust to spend

A product gap validated with user research and dogfooding

Tasks & Sequence

Aligned stakeholders on 6 tasks and sequencing over 10+ discussions

Prioritized by dependency > user needs > company goals

Tasks & Sequence

Addressed dependencies: helped users resolve account issues so they could start messaging

Data showed that 10% of businesses had account issue and couldn't send messages

Tasks & Sequence

Followed user needs: a quick test with 2 tools to find value, build trust, and start spending

Partners offered multiple tools; we designed based on learnings from successful journeys

Tasks & Sequence

Served company goals: drove business verification to unlock future spending at scale

Moved the company's top task to the final step, so users build trust before committing
