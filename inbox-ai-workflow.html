Bulk Replies for Inbox

POCs PD · Mu Jiun Shie   CD · Sarah Chen   PM · David Park   Eng · Kevin Liu
Progress Understand → Concept → MVP Design → Development → Dogfood → Launch
Review Design · Content · Pillar · Legal

Today's Problem

Re-engagement drives conversions, but Meta's current inbox tools don't make it easy for businesses to do it at scale

Businesses can only reply one by one — most leads never get a follow-up, driving them to third-party tools

>70%

new conversations came from messaging ads

63%

conversations have no follow-up within 24 hours

~10%

use third-party tools for re-engagement

2.4×

higher conversions after re-engagement

Proposal

Introduced bulk replies for Meta Inbox desktop

Enable businesses to re-engage existing customers at scale

MVP

  • Audience: Target active advertisers
  • Use Case: Ad conversations follow-up
  • Surface: Business inbox desktop

Long Term

  • Audience: All messaging businesses
  • Use Case: Post-purchase re-engagement, broadcast
  • Surface: Cross-platform rollout

Decision & Iteration

Key finding: Users want re-engagement tools since the process is highly manual, but struggle to find the tool again after first use.
Iteration: Revise the upsell rules to show it more on main screen so users can easily re-enter the tool when needed.
